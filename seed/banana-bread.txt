= Banana Bread

# Heat oven to 350 °F. Grease an 8"×4" loaf pan.

# Sift together flour, salt, baking soda, and baking powder.

- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/4 teaspoon table salt
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

# In a large bowl, beat butter until smooth, then gradually add sugar, beating until light and fluffy (about 3 minutes).

- 1/2 cup unsalted butter
- 1 cup granulated sugar

# Beat in eggs one at a time until fully incorporated. Add mashed bananas and vanilla extract, stirring until well combined.

- 2 eggs
- 2 bananas
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

# Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients in three additions, mixing until almost combined after each addition.

# Gently fold in walnuts until just distributed.

- 1/2 cup walnuts

# Transfer batter to prepared pan and smooth top with spatula. Bake for 50-55 minutes, or until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on wire rack.
