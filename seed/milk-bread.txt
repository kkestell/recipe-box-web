= Milk Bread

# Combine ingredients in a small saucepan. Whisk until no lumps remain. Cook over low heat, whisking constantly, until thick and whisk leaves lines on bottom of pan, 3-5 minutes. Let cool to room temperature.

- 3 tablespoons water
- 3 tablespoons milk
- 2 tablespoons bread flour

# Once tangzhong has cooled, add ingredients to bread machine pan in this order:

- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 egg, beaten
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted and cooled
- cooled tangzhong
- 300 grams (2 1/2 cups) bread flour
- 2 tablespoons dry milk
- 1/4 cup honey or granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast

# Select "Basic" or "White Bread" setting, 1 pound loaf, light crust. Start machine.
