= Chocolate Mayonnaise Cake

# Cream together sugar and mayonnaise until well blended.

- 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 1/2 cups mayonnaise

# In a separate bowl, sift together cocoa, baking soda, flour, and salt.

- 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 3 teaspoons baking soda
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 3/4 teaspoon salt

# Add dry ingredients to creamed mixture alternately with water and vanilla, beginning and ending with dry ingredients.

- 1 1/2 cups water
- 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract

# Pour batter into greased and floured cake pan or pans.

# Bake at 350Â°F for about 35 minutes, until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
