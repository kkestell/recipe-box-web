= Byerly's Wild Rice Soup

# Melt butter in large saucepan. Saut√© onion until tender.

- 6 tablespoons butter or margarine
- 1 tablespoon minced onion

# Blend in flour, then gradually stir in broth. Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a boil. Boil and stir 1 minute.

- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 3 cups chicken broth

# Stir in rice and salt. Simmer about 5 minutes.

- 2 cups cooked wild rice
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

# Blend in half and half and sherry. Heat to serving temperature.

- 1 cup half and half
- 2 tablespoons dry sherry (optional)

# Garnish with snipped parsley or chives.

- Fresh parsley or chives for garnish

# Variation: Add 1/3 cup minced ham, 1/3 cup finely shredded carrot, and 3 tablespoons chopped slivered almonds with rice and salt.
