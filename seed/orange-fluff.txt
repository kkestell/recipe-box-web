= Orange Fluff

# Stir pudding powder into boiling water. Add jello and mix well. Cook according to package directions and refrigerate overnight.

- 1 6-ounce package orange jello
- 1 package vanilla pudding (not instant)
- 4 1/2 cups boiling water

# Next day, whisk or beat jello mixture until fluffy.

# Add whipped topping and whip until mixed well.

- 12 ounces frozen whipped topping

# Fold in mandarin oranges and marshmallows if using.

- 1 large can mandarin oranges, drained
- 1 cup mini marshmallows (optional)
