= Microwave Peanut Brittle

# Stir peanuts, sugar, syrup, and salt in 1 1/2-quart baking dish.

- 1 cup Raw Peanuts
- 1 cup Granulated Sugar
- 1/2 cup Corn Syrup
- 1/8 teaspoon Table Salt

# Microwave on high for 7-8 minutes, stirring after 4 minutes.

# Add butter and vanilla. Cook 1-2 minutes until mixture browns slightly.

- 1 teaspoon Unsalted Butter
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract

# Stir in baking soda until light and foamy.

- 1 teaspoon Baking Soda

# Pour onto greased cookie sheet. Cool, then break into pieces.
