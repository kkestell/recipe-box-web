= Remoulade Sauce

# In a small bowl, stir together the mayonnaise, mustard, hot sauce, paprika, garlic, scallion, lemon juice, onion powder, cayenne, pickles, and capers.

- 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon Mustard
- 1 teaspoon Lemon Juice
- 1 teaspoon Hot Sauce
- 1/2 teaspoon Paprika
- 1 each Scallions
- 1 tablespoon Dill Pickles
- 1 tablespoon Capers
- 2 each Garlic
- 1/8 teaspoon Onion Powder
- 1/8 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
- 1 teaspoon Chives
