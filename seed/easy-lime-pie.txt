= Easy Lime Pie

# Mix limeade concentrate and sweetened condensed milk in medium bowl. Whisk just until blended.

- 6 ounces frozen limeade concentrate, thawed
- 14 ounces sweetened condensed milk

# Whisk in food coloring until well mixed.

- Few drops green food coloring

# Fold in dessert topping until combined.

- 8 ounces whipped topping, room temperature

# Turn mixture into pie crust.

- 9-inch graham cracker pie crust

# Refrigerate until firm, about 3 hours.

# Garnish with additional whipped topping and chocolate curls or lime slices before serving.
