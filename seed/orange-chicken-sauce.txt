= Orange Chicken Sauce

# In a medium pot, combine base ingredients and heat for 3 minutes.

- 1 cup orange juice
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1/4 teaspoon ginger
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes

# In a small bowl, whisk cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water to form a paste. Add to orange sauce and whisk together. Continue to cook for 5 minutes, until the mixture begins to thicken.

- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons water

# Once the sauce is thickened, remove from heat and add orange zest.

- 1 tablespoon orange zest
