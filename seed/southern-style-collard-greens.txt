= Southern Style Collard Greens

# Remove and discard base stems from collard greens. Coarsely chop upper stems and leaves. Wash several times in cold water and drain thoroughly.

- 1 large bunch collard greens (about 3 pounds)

# Place 1 packed cup of greens in blender or food processor and puree. Set aside.

# Place remaining greens and ham hock in a large pot and cover with water. Bring to a rolling boil.

- 1 smoked ham hock

# Add seasonings to taste and reduce heat to medium.

- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 teaspoons minced fresh garlic
- 3 teaspoons cider vinegar
- 3 teaspoons dark corn syrup
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- Hot pepper sauce to taste

# Add pureed collards. Cover and cook for 2 hours or until cooked down and tender.
