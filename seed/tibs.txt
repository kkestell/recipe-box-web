= Tibs

# Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat, then reduce heat to medium low. Add onions and cook until caramelized, about 45 minutes.

- 5 tablespoons Unsalted Butter
- 2 cups Onion, chopped

# Add garlic, ginger, and berbere and cook for a few more minutes.

- 2 tablespoons Ginger
- 2 tablespoons Garlic
- 2 tablespoons Berbere

# Process onion mixture in a blender or food processor until almost smooth. Season to taste.

# Sear sirloin in a separate skillet over high heat.

- 1 pound Beef Sirloin

# Add onion mixture to sirloin and cook until meat is done.

# Stir in lemon juice and serve.

- 1 teaspoon Lemon Juice
- Salt
