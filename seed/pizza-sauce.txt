= Pizza Sauce

# Process tomatoes through food mill or purée with immersion blender until no chunks larger than 1/16 inch remain. Set aside.

- 1 (28-ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes

# Heat oil and butter in medium saucepan over medium-low heat until butter melts.

- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

# Add garlic, oregano, pepper flakes, and large pinch of salt. Cook, stirring frequently, until fragrant but not browned, about 3 minutes.

- 2 medium cloves garlic, grated
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 pinch red pepper flakes
- 1 teaspoon Kosher salt

# Add tomatoes, onion halves, basil sprigs, and sugar. Bring to simmer.

- Reserved pureed tomatoes
- 1 medium yellow onion, peeled and halved
- 2 (6-inch) sprigs fresh basil
- 1 teaspoon granulated sugar

# Reduce heat to lowest setting and cook, stirring occasionally, until reduced by half, about 1 hour.

# Discard onions and basil stems. Season to taste with salt.

# Cool completely before storing in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
