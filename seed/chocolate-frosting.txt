= Chocolate Frosting

# With a hand-held mixer on low, mix butter, cocoa, honey, vanilla, and confectioners sugar until combined.

- 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
- 3 tablespoons Dutch processed cocoa powder
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 125 grams (1 cup) confectioners' sugar

# Slowly add milk, one tablespoon at a time, until desired consistency is reached.

- 2-4 tablespoons whole milk
