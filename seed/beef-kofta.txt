= Beef Kofta

# Add onion, garlic, and parsley to a food processor and process until finely chopped.

- 1/2 each Yellow Onion
- 3 cloves Garlic
- 1/2 cup Parsley

# Add ground beef, cumin, coriander, turmeric, cinnamon, salt, and pepper and pulse until combined.

- 1 pound Ground Beef
- 1 1/2 teaspoons Ground Cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon Ground Coriander
- 1/4 teaspoon Ground Turmeric
- 1/4 teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
- 1 1/4 teaspoons Kosher Salt
- 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper

# Divide the mixture into 6 balls. Mold each ball over a skewer, forming a kebab approximately an inch in diameter.

# Grill until golden brown and cooked through.

# Serve with pita, tzatziki, cucumber, tomato, and red onion.
