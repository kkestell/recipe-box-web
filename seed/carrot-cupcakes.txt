= Carrot Cupcakes

# Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 12-cup muffin pan with cupcake liners.

# In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and nutmeg.

- 166 grams all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon table salt
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3/4 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

# In another bowl, whisk together oil, brown sugar, eggs, applesauce, and vanilla extract until combined. Whisk in grated carrots.

- 100 milliliters vegetable oil
- 200 grams packed brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 80 grams applesauce
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 200 grams carrots, grated

# Fold the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until just combined. Fold in walnuts and raisins if using. The batter will be slightly thick.

- 60 grams walnuts, chopped
- 75 grams raisins

# Fill the liners about 3/4 full with batter.

# Bake for 21 to 23 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

# Let the cupcakes cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely before serving.

# Serve the cupcakes warm or at room temperature, garnished with fresh berries if desired.
