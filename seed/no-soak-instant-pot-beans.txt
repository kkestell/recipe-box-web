= No-Soak Instant Pot Beans

# Rinse the beans under cold water and pick out any stones or debris.

- 2 cups Dried Beans

# Add the beans, water, salt, and ham hock or bone to the Instant Pot.

- 6 cups Water
- 1 teaspoon Table Salt
- 1 each Smoked Ham Hock

# Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 30 minutes.

# Allow the pressure to release naturally for 20 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.

# Remove the ham hock or bone and discard.

# Drain the beans and use as desired, for example in red beans and rice.
