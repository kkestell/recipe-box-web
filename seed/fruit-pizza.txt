= Fruit Pizza

# Spread sugar cookie dough 1/8 inch thick on pizza pan. Bake at 350Â°F for 10 minutes. Cool completely.

- 18 ounces sugar cookie dough

# Mix cream cheese and whipped topping until smooth. Spread over cooled crust.

- 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 8 ounces frozen whipped topping, thawed

# Arrange fruit decoratively over cream cheese layer.

- Kiwi, sliced
- Strawberries, sliced
- Bananas, sliced
- Pineapple chunks
- Fresh blueberries
- Peaches, sliced

# For glaze, combine all ingredients in small saucepan. Bring to boil and cook 1 minute, stirring constantly. Cool completely.

- 1/3 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1/2 cup orange juice
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
