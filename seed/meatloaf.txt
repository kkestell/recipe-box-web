= Meatloaf

# In a large bowl, combine bread and milk, let soak for 10 minutes, then mash with a fork to form a panade. Lightly beat egg. Finely mince onion and garlic. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix gently until just combined. Do not overmix.

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1/2 pound ground pork
- 3 slices white bread
- 1 cup milk
- 1 large egg
- 1/4 cup onion
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 1/2 teaspoons table salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground mustard
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon ground sage
- 1 each garlic

# Heat oven to 350°F. Shape mixture into a loaf and place in a 9x13-inch baking pan or loaf pan.

# Spread ketchup evenly over the top of the loaf.

- 1/2 cup Ketchup

# Bake for about 1 hour, or until center reaches 160°F.
