= Broccoli Salad

# Mix all salad ingredients together in large bowl.

- 2 bunches broccoli, cut in small pieces
- 2-3 cups green and red grapes, halved
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1 cup chopped onions
- 1 cup sliced almonds, toasted
- 3/4 pound bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled

# For dressing, whisk together all ingredients until smooth.

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar

# Add dressing to salad 30 minutes to 1 hour before serving.
