= Chocolate Cake

# Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Spray a bundt cake pan with cooking spray.

# In a large mixing bowl, combine sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

- 400 grams (2 cups) granulated sugar
- 210 grams (1 3/4 cups) all-purpose flour
- 75 grams (1 cup - 2 tablespoons) natural unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
- 1 teaspoon table salt

# Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and add eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Beat for 2 minutes at medium speed. Stir in boiling water with espresso powder. The batter will be thin.

- 2 each eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 cup water, boiling
- 2 teaspoons espresso powder

# Pour batter into the prepared pan. Bake for 35-45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.

# Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then invert onto wire rack to cool completely.
