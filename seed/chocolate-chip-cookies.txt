= Chocolate Chip Cookies

# Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.

# In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until smooth. Add both white and brown sugars gradually, beating until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes.

- 1/2 cup Unsalted Butter
- 100 grams Granulated Sugar
- 100 grams Brown Sugar

# Beat in the egg until fully incorporated, then stir in the vanilla extract until combined.

- 1 each Eggs
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract

# In a small bowl, dissolve the baking soda in hot water. Add to the butter mixture along with the salt, stirring until well combined.

- 1/2 teaspoon Baking Soda
- 1 teaspoon Water
- 1/4 teaspoon Table Salt

# Gradually stir in the flour until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips and walnuts until evenly distributed.

- 180 grams All-Purpose Flour
- 1 cup Chocolate Chips
- 1/2 cup Walnuts

# Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them 2 inches apart.

# Bake for 10 minutes, or until edges are golden brown and centers are set but still soft.

# Let cool on sheets for 5 minutes, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
