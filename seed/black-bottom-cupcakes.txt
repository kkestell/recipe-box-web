= Black Bottom Cupcakes

# Heat oven to 350°F

# Combine cream cheese, sugar, and egg until smooth. Stir in chocolate chips and set aside.

- 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 1/3 cup granulated sugar
- 1 large egg
- 6 ounces chocolate chips

# In separate bowl, mix together remaining ingredients until well combined.

- 180 grams (1 1/2 cups) all-purpose flour
- 200 grams (1 cup) granulated sugar
- 26 grams (1/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon) unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup water
- 1/3 cup vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon vinegar

# Pour chocolate batter into 18 paper-lined muffin cups, filling about 2/3 full.

# Top each with 1 heaping teaspoon of cream cheese mixture.

# Bake at 350°F for 30-35 minutes, until toothpick inserted in chocolate portion comes out clean.
