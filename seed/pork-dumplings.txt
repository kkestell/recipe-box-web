= Pork Dumplings

# Cover Shiitakes with boiling water to rehydrate. Squeeze dry and finely mince.

- 1/4 cup Dried Shiitake Mushrooms

# In a small bowl, combine shredded napa cabbage with 1/2 teaspoon salt and let sit for 10 minutes to draw out moisture. After 10 minutes, squeeze out as much liquid as possible and finely chop the cabbage.

- 3 cups Napa Cabbage
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt

# In a large bowl, combine ground pork, mushrooms, ginger, garlic, green onions, soy sauce, sesame oil, rice wine, beaten egg, black pepper, and the prepared cabbage. Mix until well combined.

- 1 pound Ground Pork
- Shiitake Mushrooms
- 1 tablespoon Fresh Ginger , minced
- 3 cloves Garlic , minced
- 4 each Green Onions , finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
- 2 teaspoons Sesame Oil
- 1 tablespoon Rice Wine
- 1 each Egg , beaten
- 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper

# Lay out dumpling wrappers. Place 1 tablespoon of filling in the center of each wrapper, moisten edges with water, and fold over to seal.

- 30-35 each Dumpling Wrappers

# To cook, heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Place dumplings flat side down and cook until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes.

- 1 tablespoon Vegetable Oil

# Add 1/2 cup water to the skillet, cover, and reduce heat to low. Cook for 6-7 minutes or until water has evaporated and dumplings are cooked through.

- 1/2 cup Water

# Alternatively, steam the dumplings in a bamboo or metal steamer for 10-12 minutes, until pork is cooked through.
