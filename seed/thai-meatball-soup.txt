= Thai Meatball Soup

# Grate ginger, garlic, and jalapeño using small holes of box grater or Microplane. Place half in large bowl and reserve remainder for the soup.

- 25 grams fresh ginger, peeled
- 3 cloves garlic, peeled
- 1 each jalapeño

# To bowl with grated mixture, add ground meat, cilantro, fish sauce, salt, panko, and beaten egg. Mix gently to combine.

- 1 pound ground chicken or pork
- 1/2 bunch cilantro, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon fish sauce
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
- 1 large egg, beaten

# Form into 2-inch meatballs. Place on lined baking sheet and bake at 425°F until golden and cooked through, 12-15 minutes.

# Heat oil in large Dutch oven over medium heat. Sauté reserved ginger mixture with curry paste until fragrant, 1 minute.

- 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
- Reserved ginger mixture
- 1 tablespoon Thai curry paste

# Add stock, coconut milk, lemongrass, sugar, and fish sauce. Bring to simmer. Add baked meatballs to pot and simmer 2-3 minutes to meld flavors.

- 2 cups chicken stock
- 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
- 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised and cut into 4-inch pieces
- 1/2 teaspoon granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon fish sauce

# Remove from heat. Stir in spinach and lime juice.

- 5 ounces baby spinach
- 1 tablespoon lime juice

# Serve over rice, garnished with cilantro leaves and lime wedges.

- White or brown rice
- Cilantro leaves
- Lime wedges
