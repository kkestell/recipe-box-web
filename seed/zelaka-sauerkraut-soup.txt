= Zelňačka (Sauerkraut Soup)

# Heat a heavy-bottomed saucepan over low heat. Add fat and onions, stirring frequently until onions start to caramelize; about 45 minutes.

- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 2 tablespoons lard or neutral oil
- 3 each onions, finely chopped

# Add flour, paprika, and caraway seeds. Sauté for 30-60 seconds, stirring constantly.

- 40 grams all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon Hungarian sweet paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon caraway seeds, crushed

# Whisk in broth and bring to a boil.

- 6 cups chicken broth

# Add sauerkraut (cut if strands are long), potatoes, and bay leaves. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.

- 16 ounces sauerkraut, drained (reserve brine)
- 4 each Yukon gold potatoes, cut into 1/2" cubes
- 2 each bay leaves

# Season the soup, adjusting acidity with reserved sauerkraut brine if needed.

- 1/2 tablespoon fine sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

# Pan-fry the sausage in a teaspoon of additional fat until browned, then add to soup and stir.

- 8 ounces Polish kielbasa, sliced into rounds
- 1 teaspoon lard or neutral oil, for frying
