= Daffodil Cake

# Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gradually add sugar and salt, beating until well combined.

- 6 large egg whites
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

# Combine cream of tartar with cake flour, then gently fold into beaten egg whites. Fold in vanilla extract.

- 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1/2 cup cake flour
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

# In a separate bowl, beat egg yolks until light and fluffy. Add sugar and cold water, beating until well combined.

- 6 large egg yolks
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons cold water

# Sift together cake flour and baking powder, then fold into yolk mixture along with vanilla.

- 1/2 cup cake flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
