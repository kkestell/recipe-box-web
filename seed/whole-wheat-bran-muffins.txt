= Whole Wheat Bran Muffins

# Preheat oven to 425°F and line a 12-count muffin pan.

# Whisk flour, wheat bran, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon in large bowl.

- 1 1/2 cups Whole Wheat Flour
- 1 cup Wheat Bran
- 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking Soda
- 1/2 teaspoon Table Salt
- 1/2 teaspoon Ground Cinnamon

# Whisk egg, honey, melted coconut oil, bananas, vanilla, and milk in medium bowl. Pour into dry ingredients and whisk to combine. Fold in raisins.

- 1 each Egg
- 1/2 cup Honey
- 1/3 cup Coconut Oil, melted
- 2 each Bananas, frozen, thawed, and mashed
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
- 1 cup Milk
- 2/3 cup Raisins

# Divide batter evenly across all 12 muffin cups.

# Bake 5 minutes at 425°F, then reduce oven temperature to 350°F and bake 14-15 minutes more until toothpick comes out clean.

# Cool 5 minutes in pan, then transfer to wire rack.

# Store covered at room temperature for 3 days or refrigerate up to 1 week.
